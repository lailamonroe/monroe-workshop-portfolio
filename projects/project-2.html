Campaign / Art Direction / Brand Design

poppi mini

A playful cmockup ampaign concept built to introduce poppi’s mini can in a way that feels bold, benefits-led, and instantly snackable for modern consumers.

The mockup campagin was created as my final project in my Persuasive Media Writing and Production class at the University o fWisconsin-Milwaukee.

Overview

poppi mini is a campaign concept created to promote a smaller-format version of poppi’s prebiotic soda. Poppi mini's was designed to highlight the product’s flavor, functionality, and playful personality through bright visuals, punchy copy, and a tone that feels light, modern, and easy to connect with while being mini.

Rather than treating the mini can like just a smaller package, the concept positions it as a fun, convenient extension of the brand; one that still delivers big flavor and real benefits in a more compact format.

Project Details

  • Brand poppi
  • Role Art Direction, Design, Copy
  • Audience Gen Z + Millennials
  • Tools Adobe Creative Suite
  • Type Campaign Concept

The Challenge

How do you make a new product launch feel both functional and fun?

Functional beverage advertising can easily lean too clinical or too generic as this industry is oversaturated. For poppi minis, the challenge was to keep the product’s health benefits visible while still using poppi’s known playful and approachable personality. Additionally, offering a new product that offers a new benefit, but still has the features/benefits of the orginal sized-can.

The creative direction focused on making the mini can feel exciting, stylish, and easy to understand at a glance. To create something that could fit naturally into Poppi's mission.

Creative Strategy

A mockup campaign built on personality, benefits, and visual energy.

Audience

Gen Z and Millennial consumers who are drawn to wellness products that still feel culturally current, aesthetic, and easy to incorporate into everyday life.

Tone

Bright, playful, casual, and confident. The messaging keeps things benefit-led without losing the brand’s fun, down-to-earth voice as Poppi's mission is to change the soda industry.

Concept

The mini can is framed as small in size but big in flavor and function. It's a compact extension of the brand that still delivers on taste and gut-health appeal.

Goal

Create a launch direction that makes the new format feel instantly recognizable, visually exciting, and worth trying across multiple touchpoints. Poppi wasn't focusing on the power this particular product had, therefore an oppurtunity to showcase the importance of healthy mini soda came to life.

Visual Direction

Designed to feel bubbly, bold, and instantly scroll-stopping.

The visual system leans into bright color, bold type, cheerful composition, and energetic product placement. Something poppi is known for. Each execution was designed to feel quick, appealing, and easy to read while still carrying a strong brand personality.

The result is a campaign direction that feels fun and polished, balancing lifestyle appeal with product clarity. Making the product feel culturally relevant and honestly, a neccessity.

Takeaway

This project reflects my interest in playful, strategy-led brand storytelling.

poppi mini gave me the chance to build a campaign world that feels visually fun while still grounded in product insight. It pushed me to think carefully about how to communicate benefits, personality, and format all at once. Addtionally, I worked alongside a partner, which taught me how to be collaborative as an Art Director.

It also reflects a side of my creative style that enjoys bright visual systems, sharp copy, and design that feels both polished and approachable. It added to my interest in culture marketing and commerical print design.
